About G2G

Game to Gain (G2G) is a video game matchmaking service that aims to foster the growth of players' soft skills in a low pressure environment. Players are able to create or join game rooms, where they will be automatically connected to a Discord voice channel to communicate with their team. Players are urged to help each other improve by providing feedback on their team members' soft skills and technical skills. Join the G2G community today!
